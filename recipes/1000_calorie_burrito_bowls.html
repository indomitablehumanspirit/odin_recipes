1000 Calorie burrito bowls

Description

A meal prep ready 1000 calorie burrito bowl by Josh Cortis. Perfect for people who are trying to bulk up and need to hit their calorie goal

Ingredients

  • 1135g boneless, skinless, chicken thighs
  • 15g oil
  • 6g paprika
  • 6g onion powder
  • 6g cumin
  • 6g onion powder
  • 6g garlic powder
  • 6g ancho chili powder

Instructions

Rice

  1. Cook 3 cups of rice (dry).

Chicken

  1. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. In a large bowl make marinade, mixing oil, orange jice, lime juice, and all of the seasonings.
  3. Add chicken and coat all surfaces. Optionally marinade chicken for an hour or two first.

Vegetables

  1. Wash and cut potatoes into a large dice.
  2. Toss diced potatoes with 1 tbsp of oil and salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Cook potatoes in air fryer at 400F for 15 minutes.
  4. Cut bell peppers and onion into thin slices.
  5. Roughly chop cilantro into smaller pieces.
  6. Cook peppers and onions, heat a large skillet over medium high heat and add 1 tsbp of oil.
